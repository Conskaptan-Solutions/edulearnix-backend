Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2, 2025

Welcome to ConsKaptan ("we," "our," or "us"). This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on our websites (including Resource Kaptan and Job Kaptan), and your choices regarding their use. By using our services, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the site. It enables the website to remember your actions and preferences (such as login details, language, or display preferences) over a period of time, so you don’t have to keep re-entering them whenever you come back to the site.

How do we use cookies?

ConsKaptan uses cookies for several key purposes to enhance your experience and ensure the functionality of our platforms:

  • Authentication: We use cookies to identify our administrators when they log in to our secure dashboards on both the Resource Kaptan and Job Kaptan platforms. These cookies are essential for security and allow authorized personnel to manage content.
  • Functionality: Cookies help us remember preferences you have set. For example, when you "like" a resource, this information might be stored to create a more personalized and consistent experience for you on subsequent visits.
  • Analytics: We use third-party services like Google Analytics to collect anonymous information about how users interact with our site. This includes which pages are most popular, how much time is spent on the site, and how users navigate between our different services. This data is vital for helping us improve our content and user experience.
  • Session Management: For our administrators, we use cookies to manage login sessions and enforce our security policy of limiting concurrent sessions across devices.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function. They include cookies for admin authentication and session management. Our platforms cannot function securely without them.
  • Performance Cookies: These cookies collect anonymous, aggregated data about how you use our website. They help us understand traffic patterns and improve performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our website to remember choices you have made to provide a more personal experience.

How to control cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

However, please be aware that if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site, and some services and functionalities—especially administrator login—may not work.
